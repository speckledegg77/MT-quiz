### Storage buckets
- `audio`
- `images`

### Media path rule
Store bucket-relative paths only in the DB.

Examples:
- `2026-02-17/audio-008.mp3`
- `2026-02-17/image-003.png`

Do not store:
- a leading `/`
- `audio/` or `images/`
- a full URL

## Known working rules for future chats
